Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical ROI for a commercial solar installation?

Most of our commercial clients see a return on investment within 3-5 years, depending on local energy costs and available tax incentives. Our AI-driven efficiency systems often shorten this timeframe by an additional 15%.

Do you handle international deployments?

Absolutely. Stackly has operations in over 50 countries. We manage end-to-end logistics, local regulatory compliance, and on-site engineering teams regardless of your global location.

How does your Smart Grid AI actually work?

Our proprietary software uses predictive machine learning to anticipate energy consumption spikes based on historical data, weather patterns, and operational schedules. It then automatically routes stored battery power or grid power precisely where and when it's needed.

What happens on cloudy or windless days?

Our installations utilize massive, utility-scale solid-state battery storage systems. Excess energy generated during peak conditions is stored with near-zero degradation and automatically deployed during low-generation periods.
